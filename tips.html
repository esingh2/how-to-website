Tips for Perfect Cookies

Getting Started

  • These tips will help you bake cookies like a pro
  • Follow them while preparing, mixing, shaping, and baking
  • Use quality ingredients for best results
  • Measure carefully to ensure consistency

7 Tips & Tricks

  1. Use room temperature butter for even texture
  2. Measure ingredients accurately
  3. Don’t overmix the dough
  4. Chill dough for thicker cookies
  5. Leave space between cookies on the sheet
  6. Bake until edges are golden, not the center
  7. Cool cookies completely before storing

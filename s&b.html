Shaping and Baking

Preparation:

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • Line baking sheets with parchment paper
  • Chill dough if needed for thicker cookies
  • Have cookie dough scoops or spoon ready
  • Clean work surface and utensils
  • Set aside a cooling rack

Steps:

  1. Scoop dough onto prepared baking sheets
  2. Leave space between cookies to spread
  3. Flatten slightly if desiredt
  4. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden
  5. Remove from oven and let cool on sheet 2–3 minutes
  6. Transfer to cooling rack to finish cooling

Cookie Shapes

  • Round balls (classic chocolate chip)
  • Flat discs (thin and crispy)
  • Thumbprint (for jam-filled cookies)
  • Bars (slice after baking)
  • Cut-outs (fun shapes with cookie cutters)

Cooling and Storage

Preparation:

  • Clear a flat surface for cooling
  • Have cooling racks ready
  • Prepare airtight containers for storage
  • Line containers with parchment paper if stacking
  • Ensure cookies have enough space to cool without touching

Steps:

  1. Remove cookies from baking sheet after 2–3 minutes
  2. Place cookies on cooling racks to cool completely
  3. Do not stack cookies while warm
  4. Let cookies cool for at least 10–15 minutes
  5. Check that cookies are firm before moving to containers
  6. Rotate cookies on the rack if needed for even cooling

Storage Tips

  • Store cooled cookies in airtight containers
  • Use parchment paper between layers to prevent sticking
  • Keep at room temperature for 3–5 days
  • Freeze for longer storage (up to 3 months)
  • Reheat slightly for a fresh-baked texture
